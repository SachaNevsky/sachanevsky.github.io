PoE 2 map regex

General filter:

"([3-9].|1..)%.*rar..(?!ch)|quan|([3-9].)%.*ed.pa"

High rarity and quantity:

"([6-9].|1..)%.*ity..(?!ch)" "(1[5-9]|2.)%.*qu"

High waystone (400%+):

"ance.*([4-9]..)%"

Rare mobs and quant:

"([3-9].)%.*are.m" "(1[5-9]|2.)%.*quan"

Rarity and rare mobs:

"([6-9].|1..)%.*rar..(?!ch)" "([4-9].)%.*are.m"

Rarity or rare mobs:

"nc.*rar..(?!ch)" "e.1"

High pack size:

"([4-9].)%.*ed.pa"

Rarity or rare mobs and pack size:

"nc.*rar..(?!ch)" "mon.*packs"
